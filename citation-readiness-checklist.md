# Citation Readiness Checklist

A practical checklist for applying the AI Citation Readiness Framework.

Use this checklist to audit an organization, expert or brand before AEO implementation.

---

## Dimension 1 — Clarity

The entity is clear when AI systems can identify who or what it is without guessing.

- [ ] The entity has one consistent, canonical name used across all sources.
- [ ] The entity has a clear, one-sentence description of what it does and for whom.
- [ ] The entity is unambiguously distinguishable from similarly named organizations or people.
- [ ] The canonical website URL is consistent across all profiles and structured data.
- [ ] The founder, author or key person is explicitly connected to the entity where relevant.

---

## Dimension 2 — Consistency

The entity is consistent when its core claims are aligned across its own website, structured data and external profiles.

- [ ] The organization or expert description is consistent across website, profiles and structured data.
- [ ] The founder, author or key person description is consistent across profiles and JSON-LD where relevant.
- [ ] No conflicting roles, dates, names or claims exist across sources.
- [ ] Core claims use the same terminology and meaning across all important sources.
- [ ] `sameAs` references point to active, consistent profiles that describe the same entity.

---

## Dimension 3 — Verifiability

The entity is verifiable when external, public and machine-readable signals confirm that it exists and is credible.

- [ ] Structured data is implemented and valid.
- [ ] Relevant external profiles exist and are linked where appropriate.
- [ ] Public authority anchors exist, such as professional profiles, repositories, research profiles, author profiles or official databases.
- [ ] External mentions or citations from third-party sources exist.
- [ ] The entity can be found and correctly described by at least two AI systems.

---

## Score Interpretation

Count completed items.

Maximum score: 15 completed items.

| Completed items | AI Citation Readiness Score | Status |
|---:|---:|---|
| 0–4 | 0–25 | Invisible |
| 5–8 | 26–50 | Recognizable |
| 9–12 | 51–75 | Citable |
| 13–15 | 76–100 | Answer-Ready |

---

## Status Definitions

### Invisible

AI systems cannot reliably identify or recommend the entity.

### Recognizable

AI systems may find the entity, but cannot reliably cite or recommend it.

### Citable

AI systems can identify and cite the entity, but consistency or verifiability gaps remain.

### Answer-Ready

AI systems can understand, cite and recommend the entity with confidence.
