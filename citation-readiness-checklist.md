Citation Readiness Checklist

A practical checklist for applying the AI Citation Readiness Framework. Use this to audit an organization, expert or brand before AEO implementation.

Dimension 1 — Clarity

 The entity has one consistent, canonical name used across all sources
 The entity has a clear, one-sentence description of what it does and for whom
 The entity is unambiguously distinguishable from similarly named organizations or people
 The canonical website URL is consistent across all profiles and structured data
 The founder or key person is explicitly connected to the organization

Dimension 2 — Consistency

 The organization description is identical across website, LinkedIn, Crunchbase and structured data
 The founder description is identical across all profiles and JSON-LD
 No conflicting roles, dates or claims exist across sources
 Core claims use the same terminology and sentence structure everywhere
 The sameAs references point to active, consistent profiles

Dimension 3 — Verifiability

 Structured data (JSON-LD) is implemented and valid
 At least three external profiles exist and are linked via sameAs
 GitHub, ORCID or equivalent authority anchors are present
 External mentions or citations from third-party sources exist
 The entity can be found and correctly described by at least two AI systems
